Explore China's Natural Landscapes

Guilin's Karst Mountains and Li River

Introduction: Famous for its stunning karst peaks and serene Li River, Guilin offers one of the most iconic landscapes in China.

Experience: Take a bamboo raft ride or a river cruise to enjoy the breathtaking views.

Huangshan (Yellow Mountain)

Introduction: Known for its "Four Wonders"—oddly-shaped pine trees, spectacular peaks, sea of clouds, and hot springs—Huangshan is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Experience: Hike to the summit to witness sunrises above the sea of clouds.

Jiuzhaigou Valley

Introduction: A fairyland of turquoise lakes, cascading waterfalls, and snow-capped mountains, Jiuzhaigou is a paradise for nature lovers.

Experience: Stroll along wooden paths to immerse yourself in the natural beauty.
