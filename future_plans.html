My Future Plans

Career Aspirations

As a student passionate about computer science, I am determined to deepen my knowledge in this field and pursue a career that allows me to make meaningful contributions through technology. My future plans include:

  • Pursuing higher education in computer science, with a focus on areas like artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, or software development.
  • Participating in research projects and internships to gain practical experience and explore cutting-edge technologies.
  • Developing innovative software or tools that can make a positive impact on people's lives.

Goals for This Website

I created this website to share the beauty of Chinese culture with the world. Through this platform, I hope to achieve the following:

  • Encourage more people to learn about Chinese festivals, art, food, and traditions.
  • Provide an engaging and interactive way to explore cultural heritage.
  • Showcase my technical skills and creativity as I continue to learn and grow in web development.

Message to Visitors

I truly hope you enjoy browsing this website and discovering the unique aspects of Chinese culture. It has been a labor of love and a valuable learning experience for me. Your feedback and support mean a lot, as they inspire me to keep improving and sharing more with you.
