Discover China's Modern Cities

Beijing

Introduction: As the capital of China, Beijing blends ancient history with modern innovations. Iconic landmarks like the Forbidden City stand alongside cutting-edge architecture such as the CCTV Headquarters.

Highlights: Visit the futuristic Olympic Village or stroll through traditional hutongs to experience the perfect blend of past and present.

Shanghai

Introduction: Shanghai is known for its vibrant skyline featuring the Oriental Pearl Tower and its bustling Bund district. It is a city where East meets West seamlessly.

Highlights: Explore the historic French Concession, or marvel at the view from the Shanghai Tower, one of the tallest buildings in the world.

Shenzhen

Introduction: Known as China’s tech hub, Shenzhen is a symbol of rapid urban development. It features stunning modern skyscrapers and innovative tech parks.

Highlights: Visit the vibrant Huaqiangbei electronics market or relax in one of the city’s many green spaces like Lianhuashan Park.
