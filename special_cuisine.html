Special Cuisine

Four Famous Chinese Cuisines

Sichuan Cuisine: Famous for its bold and spicy flavors, featuring dishes like Kung Pao Chicken and Mapo Tofu.

Cantonese Cuisine: Renowned for its fresh ingredients and light, natural flavors, with dishes like dim sum and roast duck.

Shandong Cuisine: Known for its rich and salty flavors, using techniques like braising and roasting.

Jiangsu Cuisine: Highlights refined presentation and balanced, sweet flavors, featuring dishes like braised pork.
