Language & Script

Origin of Chinese Characters

The Chinese writing system is one of the oldest in the world, originating over 3,000 years ago. Early inscriptions were found on oracle bones, used in divination practices. Over time, the script evolved into different styles, such as seal script, clerical script, and modern script.

Calligraphy and Writing Art

Chinese calligraphy is not just a writing system but a unique form of artistic expression. The brushstrokes convey the writer's emotions and are highly valued for their aesthetic beauty.
